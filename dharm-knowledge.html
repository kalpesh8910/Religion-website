    Shri Ram Parichay

    Aavo Ramnavmiye Shri Ramno parichay kariye.

    • Name: Shri Ramchandra Dasharathji
    • Parents: Kaushalya and Dasharath
    • Birthplace: Ayodhya
    • Brothers: Bharat, Lakshman, Shatrughna
    • Wife: Sita
    • Guru: Vashishtha, Vishwamitra
    • Ishta Deva: Shiva
    • Avtar: 7th Avtar of Vishnu Bhagwan

    Kya Aur Kitne (Ank 7)

    • Saat yog: Jnan, Karma, Bhakti, Dhyan, Raja, Hatha, Sahaj.
    • Saat bhut: Bhut, Pret, Pishach, Kushmanda, Brahmarakshas, Vetal, Kshetrapal.
    • Saat vayu: Pravaha, Avaha, Udvaha, Samvaha, Vivaha, Parivaha, Paravaha.
    • Saat dweep: Jambu, Plaksha, Kusha, Shalmali, Krauncha, Shaka, Pushkara.
    • Saat patal: Atal, Vital, Sutal, Talatal, Mahatal, Patal, Rasatal.
    • Saat lok: Bhulok, Bhuvarlok, Swarglok, Maharlok, Janlok, Tapolok, Satyalok (Brahmalok).
    • Saat samudra: Kshirasagar, Dadhisagar, Ghritasagar, Ikshuras, Madhu, Madira, and water.
    • Saat parvat: Sumeru, Kailash, Malaya, Himalaya, Udayachal, Astachal, and Gandhamadan.
    • Sapt Puri: Ayodhya, Mathura, Maya (Haridwar), Kashi, Kanchi, Avantika (Ujjain), Dwarka.

    Ank 9

    • Navadha Bhakti: Shravan, Kirtan, Smaran, Padasevan, Archana, Vandana, Dasya, Sakhya, Atmanivedan.
    • Nau Vidhi: Paksha, Mahapaksha, Shankha, Makara, Kachhapa, Kukkuta, Mukunda, Neela, Badava.
    • Navdurga: Shailaputri, Brahmacharini, Chandraghanta, Kushmanda, Skanda Mata, Katyayani, Kalaratri, Mahagauri, Siddhidatri.
    • Other names: Kali, Karatyayani, Ishani, Chamunda, Mardini, Bhadrakali, Bhadra, Tvarita, Vaishnavi.

    Ank 10

    • Das Disha: Purva, Paschim, Uttar, Dakshin, Vayavya, Nairritya, Agneya, Ishana and directions of guardians.
    • Dashavatara: Matsya, Kurma, Varaha, Narasimha, Vamana, Parashu, Rama, Krishna, Buddha, Kalki.
    • Brahma ke das manas putra: Marichi, Atri, Angira, Pulaha, Kratu, Pulastya, Pracheta, Bhrigu, Narada, Vashishtha.
    • Sadhuo ke das sampradaya: Giri, Parvat, Sagar, Puri, Bharati, Saraswati, Van, Aranya, Tirtha, Ashrama.
    • Das Mahavidya: Kali, Tara, Lalita Tripurasundari, Bhuvaneshwari, Bhairavi, Chinnamasta, Dhumavati, Bagalamukhi, Matangi, Kamala.
