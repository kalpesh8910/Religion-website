    Roj krvano vahivat (Nari)

    To be chanted while lying in bed:

    Matru Devo Bhava, Pitru Devo Bhava

    Kuldevi Bhavani Mata Namah

    Gotra Guru Vashishtha Rishi

    🌸 Names of Five Satis:

    • Ahalya
    • Draupadi
    • Sita
    • Taramati
    • Mandodari

    🌿 Names of Saptarishis:

    • Vishwamitra
    • Vashishtha
    • Bharadwaja
    • Atri
    • Gautam
    • Kashyapa
    • Jamdagni

    Mantra to be chanted while looking at both palms:

    कराग्रे वसते...

    Meaning: Lakshmi at the fingertips...

    Mantra to be chanted before stepping on the ground:

    समुद्रवसने...

    Meaning: She who is clothed by oceans...

    After touching the earth, proceed with daily tasks.

    Chant the mantras for Tulsi Mata, lighting the lamp (Diva), and offering incense (Agarbatti) while performing the rituals.

    Mantra to be chanted for Tulsi Mata:

    तुलसी श्रीसखी...

    Meaning: Tulsi, companion of Shri and Shiva...

    Mantra for lighting the lamp (Diva):

    शुभं करोतु...

    Meaning: May it bring auspiciousness, health...

    Mantra for offering incense (Agarbatti):

    वनस्पतिरसोद्भूतो...

    Meaning: Born of the essence of trees...

    Mantra for Surya Bhagavan (Sun God):

    आदिदेव नमस्तुभ्यं...

    Meaning: Salutations to the primal deity, the Sun...

    Note: Do not offer water to Surya Bhagavan on an empty stomach.

    Mantra to be chanted at sunset (Gayatri Mantra):

    ॐ भूर्भुवः स्वः...

    Meaning: The Gayatri: we meditate on...

    Mantra to be chanted at bedtime:

    कृष्णाय वासुदेवाय...

    Meaning: To Krishna, Vasudeva, Hari...
